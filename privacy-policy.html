Privacy Policy

Junaid Chiropractic Manager

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Junaid Chiropractic Manager ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of all user data.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Patient personal information (name, age, gender, contact details)
  • Medical history and treatment records
  • Appointment schedules and history
  • Insurance information (if provided)
  • Emergency contact information

How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used for:

  • Managing patient appointments and schedules
  • Maintaining medical records and treatment history
  • Communicating with patients about appointments
  • Syncing appointments with Google Calendar (with user permission)
  • Generating reports and statistics for practice management

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Regular automated backups (if enabled)
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Secure Google Calendar integration

Google Calendar Integration

When you enable Google Calendar integration:

  • We only sync appointment information with your Google Calendar
  • Access is secured through OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • You can revoke access at any time
  • No sensitive medical information is synced to Google Calendar

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored information
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Delete your information
  • Disable Google Calendar integration
  • Request a copy of your data

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
